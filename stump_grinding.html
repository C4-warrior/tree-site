Stump Grinding Services

Reclaim Your Landscape

After a tree has been removed, the remaining stump can be an unsightly obstacle and a safety hazard. Our professional stump grinding service is the most effective way to remove it completely.

Using state-of-the-art equipment, we grind the stump down below ground level, destroying the root ball and preventing regrowth. The resulting wood chips can be used as mulch for your garden or we can haul them away, leaving you with a clean, level surface ready for new landscaping, paving, or lawn.
